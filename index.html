Welcome!

Who we are?

A student-driven community at Deakin University, GIFT City, India that aims to enhance the coding and analytics culture across the entire institution. We plan to achieve this by offering regular demonstrations covering topics ranging from beginner to advanced levels.

What is our aim?

To elevate the coding and analytics environment and practices across our campus.

Why are we here?

To develop a community, making significant contributions across diverse domains and uphold the integrity of Deakin University, GIFT City, India as a technical institution.
